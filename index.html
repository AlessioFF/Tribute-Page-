Nikola Tesla

The man who revolutionized electricity

Timeline of Tesla's Life:

  • 1856 – Born in Smiljan, Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia)
  • 1881 – Moves to Budapest and works for the Budapest Telephone Exchange
  • 1884 – Moves to the United States and begins working for Thomas Edison
  • 1887 – Develops the induction motor, patented by Tesla
  • 1891 – Invents the Tesla Coil, becomes a U.S. citizen
  • 1893 – Demonstrates AC power at the World’s Fair in Chicago
  • 1901 – Begins building the Wardenclyffe Tower for wireless power transmission
  • 1943 – Dies in New York City at age 86

"If you want to find the secrets of the universe, think in terms of energy, frequency, and vibration." - Nikola Tesla
